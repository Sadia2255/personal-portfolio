Flask Weather App

Tweet Textbot

I built a Java Textbot using a Markov Chain model trained on a dataset of tweets to generate text blocks or tweets of a specified length. The bot processed and cleaned the tweet file, mapped the probability distribution of adjacent words, and used this to generate coherent text based on a starting word until the desired length was reached.

Rock, Paper, Scissors
